FAQ

Common questions

Is CricHeroes really free?
Yes. Live scoring and league management have been free since 2016 — no match limits, no player limits, no expiry. Paid plans exist for streaming, broadcast tools and Pro analytics, but the core product is free forever.
I'm on CricClubs. How do I switch?
We handle the entire migration — match history, player profiles, team data — for free. Email partnership@cricheroes.in and our team will take care of everything.
What's the difference between the league plans?
T20 ($999/yr) includes CricHeroes Stream and Ticker for your whole league. ODI ($1,999/yr) adds Capture camera and Match Centre Embed. Test (custom) is full white-label with your own app and website. See full comparison →
What is CricHeroes Pro for players?
Pro is your 24×7 personal cricket analyst — batting and bowling insights, opponent analysis, live match intelligence. Three moments: prepare smarter before matches, perform better during, analyse sharper after. Learn more →
Do you have support in North America?
Yes. We have dedicated full-time team members in the Americas. Reach us via WhatsApp, email or call — 24/7. Email partnership@cricheroes.in for North America enquiries.
Can I stream matches with CricHeroes?
Yes — from $5/match for individual use, or league-wide streaming included in the T20 plan. CricHeroes Capture (our in-house camera) makes streaming completely automatic — no crew needed.
